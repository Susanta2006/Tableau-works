# Interactive Sales Dashboard (Case Study 1)

## 📌 Overview

_This dashboard was developed to analyze the sales lifecycle of a retail organization. The primary goal is to compare regional performance against product sub-category inventory levels._

## 📊 Key Data Insights

 - Based on the visualization of approximately $2.29M in total sales:

 - Regional Leader: The South Region outperforms others with an average sale of $241.80.

 - Inventory Focus: Binders (5,974 units) and Paper (5,178 units) are the highest-moving sub-categories by volume.

 - Category Split: A balanced distribution between Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology suggests a diversified revenue stream.

## ⚙️ Technical Features

 - Metric Selection: Used a mix of absolute values (Quantity) and averages (Sales) to provide a nuanced view of performance.

 - Visual Encoding: Employed length (Bar Charts) for regional comparison to ensure high data-ink ratio and accuracy.

 - Interactivity: Integrated category legends as high-level filters for the entire dashboard.
